The Sticky Component

Sticky Component

This is the sticky component. It sticks things to the top of your screen then removes them at the bottom of the content area.

Here's how it works


<section class="sticky-container">

  <article>

    this is the body content the the sticky element should stay next to.

  </article>

						
  <aside data-sticky="sticky-container">

    This is the content your want stuck to the top.	

  </aside>

</section>
